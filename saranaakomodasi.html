Sanur Beach

Sanur is full of historical value, and has attracted the attention of historians and archaeologists since the discovery of the oldest and written record of the existence of the island of Bali. Sanur has the meaning "Saha" and "Nuhur" which means begging to come to a place. Sanur also means "Sa" which means one and Nur which means light. The Brahmins at that time saw the pedestal of Pategaling Magalak shining so that the place where the light fell was called Sanur.
